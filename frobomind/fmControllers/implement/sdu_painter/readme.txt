University of Southern Denmark (SDU) 2015-08-21

The sdu_painter component is controls a painter implement which is capable
of actuating the nozzle of line marking spray cans. It is used for marking
of dots and lines.

The firmware for a FroboMind Controller http://www.frobomind.org/fmctrl
as well as a FroboMind interface node is available in this directory.

The sdu_painter was developed by Miichael Nielsen, Mikkel Kildemand Larsen,
Mathias Neerup and Kjeld Jensen from SDU.
